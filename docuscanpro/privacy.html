Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28

This Privacy Policy explains how Inovation (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

Support: support@inovation.app

1) Information We Collect

a) Content you create

  • Scans, photos, PDFs, text you add, signatures, and edits you apply inside the App (“User Content”).
  • By default, User Content is stored on your device and is accessible only to you, unless you choose to share it.

b) Device permissions

  • Camera: to scan documents.
  • Photos: to import images and (optionally) save exports.
  • If you deny permissions, some features may not function. You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

c) Usage and diagnostics

  • Basic app diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, performance data) may be collected to improve stability and performance.
  • These diagnostics do not intentionally include your document content.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: scanning, editing, exporting, sharing, and file management.
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs (e.g., via diagnostics).
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.

3) OCR and On-Device Processing

Where available, text recognition (OCR) and document processing are performed on-device. If the App offers optional cloud-based processing in the future (e.g., advanced conversion), we will:

  • Ask for your explicit action/consent before uploading any file,
  • Explain what is sent and why,
  • Minimize retention and protect data in transit.

4) Sharing and Exporting

When you use the share/export features, you choose where your files are sent (e.g., email, messaging apps, cloud storage). Those third parties process your data under their own policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

5) Subscriptions and Purchases

If the App offers subscriptions or in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details. Apple may provide us with limited purchase/entitlement information to unlock features.

6) Data Storage and Retention

  • User Content remains on your device unless you delete it or choose to share/export it.
  • Diagnostics data (if collected) is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for troubleshooting and improvement.

7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8) Your Choices and Rights

  • Access / Delete: You can delete documents and data within the App at any time.
  • Permissions: You can manage Camera/Photos permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Support Requests: You can contact us to ask questions about privacy and data handling.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) International Users

If you use the App from outside your country, your information may be processed where you use the App and where our service providers (if any) operate. We take steps to protect information consistent with this policy.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the App.

12) Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@inovation.app.
