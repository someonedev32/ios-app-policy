Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28

This Privacy Policy describes how DocuScan Pro (“the App”) handles information. If you have questions, contact us at support@inovation.app.

1) Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information or user data with us or any third parties. The App is designed to work offline and does not require an account.

2) On-Device Processing

All core features—including document scanning, image enhancement, cropping, adding text or signatures, PDF creation/export, and text recognition (OCR)—are performed entirely on your device. No documents, images, or extracted text are uploaded to any server.

3) Permissions

The App may request the following permissions only to provide functionality:

  • Camera: to scan documents.
  • Photos: to import images or save exported files.

Permission access is used locally on your device. You can manage permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

4) Storage

Scans and exported files are stored locally on your device. You can delete your documents at any time within the App or by removing the App from your device.

5) Tracking & Advertising

The App does not track users, does not use advertising identifiers, and does not request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission.

6) Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share your data with third parties. If you choose to export or share a file using iOS sharing options, the destination app or service will handle that file under its own privacy policy.

7) Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will change the effective date above.

8) Contact

For questions about privacy, contact: support@inovation.app
