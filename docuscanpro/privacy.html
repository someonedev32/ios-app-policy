Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28
App: DocuScan Pro
Support: support@docuscanpro.app

Summary: DocuScan Pro works fully offline. We do not collect, store, share, or sell personal data. Your scans and files stay on your device unless you choose to export/share them.

1) Data We Collect

We collect no personal data.

DocuScan Pro does not require accounts and does not use our servers, cloud sync, or a remote database. Any content you create (scans, photos, PDFs, text, edits, signatures) is stored locally on your device.

2) On-Device Processing

Document scanning, image processing, and (if available) OCR/text recognition are performed on-device. Your documents are not uploaded to us for processing.

3) Permissions

  • Camera: used to scan documents.
  • Photos: used to import images and optionally save exports.

You can deny permissions, but some features may not function. You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

4) Network, Tracking, Analytics

DocuScan Pro does not transmit your document content to any server. We do not use advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics/crash-reporting services.

If you open external links (for example, this policy page), those websites may collect data under their own policies.

5) Sharing and Exporting

If you use share/export features, you decide where your files go (e.g., Mail, Messages, Files, cloud drives). Those third parties process data under their own privacy policies. We do not control their practices.

6) Purchases (If Applicable)

If the app offers subscriptions or in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment details. Apple may provide limited entitlement information to unlock features.

7) Data Retention

Your content remains on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app typically removes locally stored app data. Device backups are controlled by your iOS settings.

8) Security

Since data stays on your device, security depends on your device protections (passcode/biometrics). We recommend enabling iOS security features.

9) Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data because the app does not collect data at all.

10) Changes to This Policy

If app functionality changes (for example, adding cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or server processing), we will update this policy and the effective date above.

11) Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at:
support@docuscanpro.app
