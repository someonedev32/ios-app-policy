Privacy Policy

App: My Beach
Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

My Beach is an offline-first application and does not use any server-side database. We do not collect, store, or process personal data from users.

Information We Collect

None. The app does not require registration and does not request personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

Local Data Storage

Any data you create in the app (for example, locally managed reservations, configurations, or preferences) is stored only on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • No third-party analytics are used.
  • No advertisements are displayed.
  • No tracking SDKs or user profiling are used.

In-App Purchases

If you purchase a subscription, the payment is processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. Apple may provide us with limited, non-personal technical information (such as subscription status) solely for the purpose of validating access to premium features.

Permissions

The app does not require access to sensitive device permissions (such as contacts, location, or camera) for its core functionality. If future features require additional permissions, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Security

Since no user data is transmitted or stored on our servers, the risk of data exposure from our side is minimal. The security of locally stored data depends on the security of your device.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects data handling (for example, adding online synchronization), this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
